About HIT-VISA

Visa application / visa extension is a nontrivial process in China. International students need to provide numerous documents and certifications to Harbin Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration. So this website is carefully designed for international students in HIT to guide them through the visa application / extention procedure.

By filling out and submitting visa application information online, this website will automatically generate Visa Application Form for you to download, which saves you a lot time.

Following the guidelines provided by this website, you will be able to prepare all the documents and materials needed for visa application. But you still need to go to Harbin Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration personally to submit your application materials, which is required by the government policy.

If You Entered China With Visa Free

If you entered China with visa free, you should have been accepted as a Chinese Language Student. if not, you need to go to Office 306 of International Student Center to apply for visa personally. Applying on this website will be considered invalid, so don't waste your time on this website.

If You Are Family Members of HIT International Students

An identification proof can be offered by International Student Center for family members of students in HIT. In this condition, you need to go to Office 306 of International Student Center to apply for visa personally. Applying on this website will be considered invalid, so don't waste your time on this website.
