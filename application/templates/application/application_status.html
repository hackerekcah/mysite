    • STEP 4:

    Submit Documents / Materials to International Student Office(ISC) for check

  • This STEP should be done 10-15 days in advance of the expiation of visa or residence permit.
  • If it meets the exams, travels, national holidays and other arrangements, please arrange your time properly, extend your visa beforehand.
  • Students should bring all the documents (which you have prepared in STEP 3) to Office 306 of ISC for documents check.
  • After documents check, Office 306 should tell you visa application fee you need to submit in STEP 5. if not, ask for it.
  • The office hour open for documents check is 8:00am - 11:00am from Monday to Friday (National holidays excepted).
  • Please make sure that your visa or residence permit is valid during your study at HIT, otherwise you will receive the penalties(at most 10,000 RMB) from the Public Security Bureau.
  • If you are not clear about your visa expiration date, please take your passport to ISC to inquire.

    • STEP 5:

    Submit documents to Harbin Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration

  • Go to Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration reception hall, submit the application materials
  • Prepare visa application fee as in STEP 4. If you forget, contact Office 306 of ISC.
  • The Bureau will take 20-30 days for visa processing, and during this time all your documents will be left in the Bureau including your passport.
  • If you need to use your passport during visa processing time, you need to ask for Identity Certification at the time you submit your documents to Harbin Municipal Public Security Bureau.
  • If you didn't ask for Identity Certification at the time you submit your documents, it is not possible to ask for it later.
  • We recommend everyone to ask for Identity Certification at the time you submit your documents. And it is free.

    • STEP 6:

    Fetch your passport 30 days after

  • Go to Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration reception hall, fetch your passport with new visa page.
  • Go to Office 306 of ISC to inqure the expiration date of this new visa.
  • You need to apply for visa again on this website 10-15 days before the expiration of visa.
