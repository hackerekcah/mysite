Application Procedure Timeline

  • Accomodation Registration

    Before you start visa application procedure, we highly recommend you to finish accomodation registration first.

    Because when you fill out the visa application form online, you need to provide your address, which should be identical to the address column on your accomodation registration form.

  • Register On HIT-VISA

    You need an account on HIT-VISA to start visa application procedure.

    If you already got one account, you can just log in.

    Your account will be valid during your study at HIT

  • Fill Out And Submit Visa Application Form Online

    After submission, website will automatically generate Foreigner Visa Dcuments Application Form (《外国人签证证件申请表》) for you.

  • Download And Print Foreigner Visa Dcuments Application Form

    After submission, website will automatically generate Foreigner Visa Dcuments Application Form (《外国人签证证件申请表》) for you.

  • Materials / Documents Preparation

    According to your application, wesite will inform you the list of documents you need to prepare.

  • Submit Documents / Materials to International Student Office(ISC) for Check

    You need to submit these documents 10-15 days before the expiration of your visa or residence permit.

  • Submit documents to Harbin Municipal Public Security Bureau Exit-Entry Administration

    .

  • Fetch your passport 30 days after

    .

  • The End

    You need to apply for visa again on this website 10-15 days before the expiration of this new visa.
